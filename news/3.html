Velocity Updates!

@everyone Hello everyone! Today we have updates on this project:
Message previews are here!

See your messages in the recent chats list! Tell your friends that dark mode is now available.

Attachments with spice

Hey, would you like some attachments with a side of spice? Attachments are now available on Velocity. Send your favorite videos, audio, or images!
Note: You are currently unable to send media such as ZIP, EXE, etc. This will be fixed sometime in a later update.

Lil bit of customisation

You can now choose between light and dark mode. Of course, light mode is the OG theme so it will be selected by default, but if you want some uniqueness you can now change it. More options for customisability may be available soon so keep your eyes wide open for that. Note: This will not sync if you logout, or use another device. Settings syncing will come in a later update.
Bug fixes:
Group Admins list is no longer grabbing data from the members list - I am NOT a admin!
More is coming soon, stay tuned for the next update.
